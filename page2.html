F&N Concepts

What students will study!

G7 - G9

Nutritions

During these 3 years, students will learn about basic information about the main substances that are essential for life. They are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, water, minerals and vitamins.  Students will be taught on how to read nutrition fact label and count calories from it.  

G10

Restaurants

In this year, students will be taught on the different types of restaurants, their characteristics and their differences. Students will be asked to survey a specific types of restaurant and create an observation report, followed by a presentation and a video of the report.

G11

Services in Restaurant

Students will learn the proper way of serving customers, as well as knowing how to fold table cloth and carry a tray with stacks of plates, glasses and tablewares properly. 

G12

Business in Restaurant

Different restaurant have different ways on how to run their business successfuly. Students will learn from Mr. Febrian Ralahalu some of the techniques that is commonly used in daily lifes. Afterward, students must research on their own techniques used by local restaurants.
