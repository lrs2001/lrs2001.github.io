Food & Nutrition

Concepts and Activities

What students will Study!!

G7 - G9

Nutritions

During these 3 years, students will learn about basic information about the main substances that are essential for life which are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, water, minerals and vitamins.  Students will be taught on how to read nutrition fact label and count calories from it.  

G10

Restaurants

In this year, students will be taught on the different types of restaurants, their characteristics and their differences. Students will be asked to survey a specific types of restaurant and create an observation report, followed by a presentation and a video of the report.

G11

Services in Restaurant

Students will learn the proper way of serving customers, as well as knowing how to fold table cloth and carry a tray with stacks of plates, glasses and tablewares properly. 

G12

Business in Restaurant

Different restaurant have different ways on how to run their business successfuly. Students will learn from Mr. Febrian Ralahalu some of the techniques that are commonly used in daily lifes. Afterward, students must research on their own, techniques used by local restaurants to achieve success.

Student's Activities

Cleaning

After cooking, students must clean the utensils that they used by themselves. This teaches the students how to be hygienic, getting them used to being clean.

Sharpen your skills

During the cooking period, students will be taught skills and techniques to cook certain dishes. Students will gradually improve their ability under the guidance of Mr. Febrian

Reflection!

At the end of the year, students must give their folder of cooking reflection to Mr. Febrian. This will teach the students to evaluate their activities during the class. This is also another way to remember fun memories during the school year with Mr. Febrian.
