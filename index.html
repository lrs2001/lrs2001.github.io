Food & Nutrition

A place for all Food Lovers

Food & Nutrition is one of selective classes that are 
available for the Springfield student to join. This is the 
class, where one could hone their cooking skill under the
guidance of Mr.Febrian, one of Springfield's cooking
teacher, or enjoy doing their hobby during school time.
